# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Judge contributions on their merit — papers, tools, and products are evaluated by the criteria in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md), not by who submits them.
- Harassment, personal attacks, and spam (including undisclosed self-promotion that ignores the quality bar) are not tolerated.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the repository maintainers, and may result in comments being removed or contributors being blocked.
